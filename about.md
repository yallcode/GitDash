# 🕐 GitDash — Time Machine for GD Creators

> **"I accidentally deleted my entire drop section."**  
> Never say that again.

---

GitDash is a version control system for the Geometry Dash level editor. Every time you save your level, GitDash silently captures a snapshot of it. No manual backups. No duplicate levels cluttering your list. Just a quiet safety net running in the background — and a **Timeline** button in your pause menu whenever you need it.

---

## How it works

Save your level → GitDash snapshots it.  
Something goes wrong → Open the pause menu → Tap **Timeline**.  
Browse your save history → Tap **Restore**.  
Done. Your level is back exactly as it was.

---

## Features

- 📸 **Silent snapshots** on every Save, Save and Play, and Save and Exit
- ⏪ **Timeline UI** in the editor pause menu — navigate with arrows, restore in one tap
- 📦 **Efficient storage** — snapshots stored per level, auto-pruned when limit is reached
- ⚙️ **Configurable** — set max snapshots per level (5–200), toggle autosave snapshots
- 🔒 **Non-destructive** — your actual saved levels list is never touched
- 🆓 **Free and open source** — MIT licensed, no ads, no tracking

---

## Settings

| Setting | Default | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Max Snapshots Per Level | 50 | How many snapshots to keep. Oldest deleted first when exceeded. |
| Snapshot on Autosave | Off | Capture a snapshot when GD's autosave fires too |
| Show Timestamps | On | Display HH:MM:SS next to relative time in the Timeline |
