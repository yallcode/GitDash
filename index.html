What it does

Every save.
Perfectly preserved.

🕐
Auto Snapshots

Every time you hit Save, GitDash silently captures your level data. No manual backups. No clutter in your levels list.

⚡
One-Tap Restore

Open the Timeline from the pause menu, browse your history with arrows, and restore any snapshot instantly.

📦
Smart Storage

Snapshots are stored efficiently. Configure how many to keep per level — oldest are auto-deleted when the limit is reached.

🔒
Non-Destructive

GitDash never touches your actual saved levels list. Your snapshots live in a separate, isolated storage directory.

⚙️
Configurable

Control max snapshots per level, enable autosave snapshots, and toggle timestamp display — all from the Geode settings menu.

🆓
Free & Open Source

MIT licensed. No paywalls, no ads, no telemetry. Built by YallCode for the GD creator community.

Usage

How to use GitDash

1
Install via Geode

Download GitDash from the Geode mod index or install the .geode file manually. Requires Geode v5.x on Geometry Dash 2.2081.

2
Build & Save as usual

Open any level in the editor and work normally. Every time you tap Save, Save and Play, or Save and Exit — GitDash silently captures a snapshot in the background.

3
Open the Timeline

Made a mistake? Open the Editor Pause Menu and tap the Timeline button in the top-right corner.

4
Browse & Restore

Use the ← → arrows to navigate your snapshot history. Each snapshot shows when it was taken and how large it is. Tap Restore to roll back instantly.
